    Baumhass 2025

    This is Baumhass, he is a quiet man who only longs to live the rest of his days in the forest. But no mater how hard he tries he can't. He is unwilling to simply abandon those who need him.

    Alistor 2025

    This is a depiction of the child Alistor and his adoptive father odisius. Odisius is perplexed by this strange and powerful child, so hell bent on vengence, and yet still in awe of so many things. Alistor has no future, he is destened to die for good only a week or two before this scene. Despite knowing this he contiues to gain skils and friends. Alistor is more a creature than a human, though his vessel is small and cute, make no mistake. Even the monster standing before him is confused.

    Advertisements

    For several years now I have been making advertisements for the red mountain game club at the collage I attend. The majority of these advertisements are for that cause. As apart of this for most of them I had a few guidelines to follow; make the printed posters a certain size, feature the logos in the bottom corner, and don't use very much ink. this last one I struggled with a little, I prefer dark and dynamic posters, however I am also capible of working within restraints.

    Game club Main Poster 2024

    This was one of the first posters I made for game club. the mascot was designed by another club member, then I put it all into My fun little poster. It still makes me smile to see my poster out at the campus semester after semester.
